Spy x Family

"Spy x Family" follows Twilight, a spy posing as a psychiatrist named Loid Forger, he has a mission whereas he has to "build a family" to infiltrate the world of a mysterious political leader, Donovan Desmond. Unknown to Twilight, his family members have their own secrets. Yor, his wife, is a deadly assassin known as the Thorn Princess, while their adopted daughter, Anya, possesses telepathic abilities. As they navigate this unconventional family dynamic, they remain oblivious to each other's true identities. The series explores the challenges of their double lives and whether they will ever discover the hidden truths within their own family, making it an engaging and humorous espionage tale.
